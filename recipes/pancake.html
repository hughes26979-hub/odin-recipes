Description

A quick guide to a breakfeast staple. These fluffy pancakes are the perfect go-to dish for a any morning.

Ingredients

  • Just add water pancake mix
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Add a cup of pancake mix into a bowl
  2. In same bowl add half-cup of water and wisk into you have a thick consistentcy
  3. Heat a pan to low-medium heat and add butter
  4. Pour a scoupful of the mix into the pan making a circular shape.
  5. Cook on each side for 2-3 minutes or until a crisp orange. (Bubbles showing are a good indication that the side is ready to flip)
  6. Remove pancake from pan and repeat
