Description

A step-by-step recipe on how to cook authetnic Cuban fried sweet plantains. This easy-to-follow recipe can be completed in less than 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Plantains
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Peel ripe plantains
  2. Cut peeled plantain into diagnoalshapes
  3. Heat a pan on medium-high heat with just enough oil to cover the bottom
  4. Shallow fry plantains, 3-5 minutes each side until golden
  5. Remove plantains from pan and season with salt
